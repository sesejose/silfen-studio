Delivery and Returns

Delivery inside of Denmark takes 1-3 business days. Delivery outside of Denmark takes 3-9 business days. The shipping time is the estimated time from when the package leaves our warehouse in Denmark until it reaches its destination. On all orders we offer a fair shipping fee depending on shipping distance. If for any reason you are not satisfied with the items purchased, you can return your purchase within 21 days for a full refund. The return period is counted from the day you receive your goods.
